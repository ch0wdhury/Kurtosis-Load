About Us

Kurtosis Paraplanning is created by analytical minds, providing exceptional analysis and research to the Statement of Advice construction and design.


Kurtosis Paraplanning are a Sydney and Melbourne based outsourced Paraplanning business providing financial advisers around Australia with a sophisticated, affordable, and local outsourced Paraplanning solution.


Kurtosis Paraplanning is a boutique Paraplanning service for Advisers that want that next edge to their Statement of Advice.

Why

The industry is lacking an analytical mindset in how we solve problems. Kurtosis Paraplanning brings a new standard of knowledge and experience to the Financial Planning industry.

What

Bringing you in depth research and modelling of various financial instruments to provide you with a holistic analysis of problems. Giving you greater opportunity with less risk.

Who

Kurtosis Paraplanning is an on-shore outsourced Paraplanning provider based in Sydney and Melbourne. Our Paraplanners have worked in the industry over the last 3-5 years and are currently studying various levels of the CFA© (Chartered Financial Analyst) program.

How

With the use of CFA©, ADFP© and DFP© along with other finance related education, our team of Paraplanners will be able to bring the competitive advantage Advisers have been looking for.
